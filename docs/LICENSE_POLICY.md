# License policy

`spikalabs-design-kit` is an MIT-licensed fork of the original `taste-skill`
project. The fork keeps upstream attribution visible while making the ownership
of spikalabs-authored modifications explicit.

## Copyright layers

| Scope | Copyright notice | License |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Original upstream `taste-skill` source and preserved substantial portions | `Copyright (c) 2026 Leonxlnx` | MIT |
| Fork-specific modifications created by spikalabs after the fork | `Copyright (c) 2026 spikalabs Co, Ltd.` | MIT |

## Policy

- Do not remove the upstream `taste-skill` attribution from `LICENSE`,
  `NOTICE.md`, or this policy file.
- Do not remove the spikalabs modification copyright notice from `LICENSE`,
  `NOTICE.md`, or this policy file.
- New changes authored for this fork should be treated as spikalabs-owned
  modifications unless a file-level notice states otherwise.
- Keep the repository license as MIT unless spikalabs explicitly approves a
  license change.
- When copying a substantial portion of this repository, include `LICENSE` and
  `NOTICE.md` so both the upstream and spikalabs notices travel with the code.

## Brand assets

The committed spikalabs symbol assets are part of the fork-specific brand and
design-kit guidance. When reusing them outside this repository, preserve the
spikalabs attribution and do not present the symbol as a third-party mark.
